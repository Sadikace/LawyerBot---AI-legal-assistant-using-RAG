LawyerBot
Kerala Legal AI Assistant · v2.0 · 2025
LawyerBot is an AI-powered legal chatbot built using Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). It answers questions on Kerala Civil Law, Criminal Law, Motor Vehicle regulations, the Indian Constitution, Cyber & IT Law, and supports Custom Integration — allowing universities, colleges, and organizations to embed their own institutional rules for context-aware legal guidance.
